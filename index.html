Hello, My name is Steven. I paint houses for a living and I am a youth minister on the side. I have had some big questions running through my mind lately. I have a burning passion in my heart to understand who Christ is and how I should live my life to serve Him. Also, because of this passion, I want others to know about Christ. And why not? If I am willing to go all out for Jesus, the man from Nazareth, why shouldn't I share this hope with all those I come in contact with? That leads to the ultimate question that persists within me: why donâ€™t the majority of Christians evangelize? According to Bible.org, Ninety-five percent of all Christians have never won a soul to Christ. Eighty percent of all Christians do not consistently witness for Christ, and less than two percent are involved in the ministry of evangelism.

Why? What is the reason that keeps the majority of Christians from going out and witnessing to the lost? Whether it be to strangers, friends or coworkers why is it so difficult for Christians to proclaim the gospel outside of church and bible studies? Why are the majority of Christians Out of Commission?

After sharing my heart with friend and filmmaker Sam Ramsey, We decided to make a documentary. The documentary will explore questions we both have about Christianity and evangelism. We will conduct interviews with pastors, missionaries, evangelists, and people who have been led to Christ through the great commission.

We want to ask challenging questions with the intent of inspiring the viewer to be obedient to Christ in a way that ultimately glorifies His name. After all, evangelism is about pointing people to our wonderful Savior. We truly desire this film to be Christ centered. We aim to glorify Christ above all with this film, not to glorify any man. We have no intention of pushing any one doctrine. We have no intention of implying there is one way to evangelize or that everyone should do it the way we do. Our intention is to focus the film on Christ and our deeply rooted need for a savior. We look forward to the coming days as we meet new people and develop relationships that point our lives and the lives of those we come in contact with to Christ Jesus.
